Dining on the Island

Seaside Grille

Nestled on the edge of Taniti Island’s pristine beach, Seaside Grill offers a laid-back yet inviting atmosphere where guests can enjoy the island’s gentle ocean breeze while savoring delicious comfort food. The restaurant boasts a cozy, open-air setting with wooden tables under thatched umbrellas, perfect for a casual dining experience. The menu highlights fresh seafood, tropical fruit-infused salads, and grilled meats, all with a local twist. Whether you're lounging by the beach or dipping your toes in the sand, Seaside Grill promises a relaxed, fun dining experience ideal for families and beach lovers alike.

The Coral Table

For a more refined yet still relaxed dining option, The Coral Table offers an upscale bistro ambiance featuring contemporary coastal cuisine. The intimate indoor dining room is decorated with coral-inspired designs and soft lighting, creating an inviting, elegant space. Guests can enjoy a variety of gourmet dishes with an emphasis on fresh, sustainable local ingredients, such as ceviche, lobster ravioli, and seasonal tropical fruits paired with delicate sauces. The Coral Table is perfect for couples looking for a romantic dinner with a touch of sophistication, as well as those who appreciate a more elevated island dining experience.

Mango Breeze Café

Mango Breeze Café is Taniti’s go-to spot for a casual yet vibrant breakfast or lunch. Set in a colorful, open-air café, the vibe is laid-back with a splash of island flair. Guests can start their day with fresh smoothies, tropical fruit bowls, or locally sourced pastries. For lunch, the café serves hearty sandwiches, tacos, and light salads that capture the bright flavors of the island. The café's cheerful atmosphere, accented with bright mango hues and shaded seating, invites guests to enjoy a relaxed meal while taking in the beauty of the surrounding gardens and ocean views.

Tiki Tide

Tiki Tide combines island fun with upscale dining, offering an unforgettable experience for those seeking tropical-inspired dishes in a vibrant, tiki-themed setting. This open-air restaurant features bamboo décor, dim tiki torches, and an immersive island experience with live music on certain nights. The menu highlights dishes influenced by Polynesian and Caribbean cuisines, such as grilled fish skewers, tropical fruit salsas, and exotic cocktails. Tiki Tide is perfect for those looking to enjoy a lively evening with delicious food and a festive atmosphere, whether for an anniversary dinner or a celebratory gathering with friends.

The Palms Fine Dining

For the ultimate upscale dining experience on Taniti, The Palms Fine Dining delivers world-class cuisine in a sophisticated setting. The restaurant is tucked away within a lush tropical garden, with floor-to-ceiling windows offering sweeping views of the ocean. The menu is a carefully curated selection of French and Mediterranean-inspired dishes, including delicate seafood platters, slow-roasted meats, and exquisite desserts. Impeccable service and an extensive wine list elevate the experience, making The Palms Fine Dining perfect for guests celebrating a special occasion or simply looking for a luxurious meal surrounded by elegance and tranquility.
