Frequently Requested Information

What is the standard voltage for power outlets on the island?

Power outlets around the island are standardized at 120 volts (the same as the United States).

Is alcohol allowed to be served and consumer on the island?

Yes, alcohol is allowed on the island, but is only allowed to be served or sold between the hours of 9:00 am and midnight.

What is the legal drinking age on Taniti?

The drinking age on Taniti is 18 years old.

Is English commonly spoken on the island?

While Enlish is not the primary language on Taniti, many people, especially younger Tanitians, will speak fluent English. Very little Enlish is spoken in more rural areas of the island, especially by older residents.

Is there access to medical facilities on the island?

There is one hospital and several clinics on the island for anyone seeking medical attention. Many of the hospital employees are multilingual.

Should visitors be worried about crime on the island?

Violent crime is incredibly rare on Taniti. As tourism increases there is a small rise in reports of petty crimes such as pickpocketing.

Are holidays observed in Taniti?

Taniti enjoys a large number of national holiays. Visitors should plan accordingly as many tourist attractions and restuarants close on holidays.

What type of currency is used on the island?

Taniti uses the U.S. dollar as its currency, but many businesses will also accept euros and yen. Several banks facilitate currency exchange, and many businesses accept major credit cards.
