Experiences

Volcano Summit Expedition: A Journey to Mount Kaloa

Embark on an unforgettable adventure with the Volcano Summit Expedition, where guests are invited to hike to the top of Mount Kaloa, the active volcano nestled within Taniti Island’s majestic Kaloa Mountain Range. This guided trek takes you through lush rainforests, along rugged volcanic terrain, and up to the summit, offering stunning panoramic views of the island and surrounding Pacific Ocean. The expedition lasts approximately six hours, with a moderate-to-challenging difficulty level, ensuring a rewarding experience for those in good physical condition. Along the way, hikers will pass steaming vents, bubbling sulfur springs, and ancient lava flows, all while learning about the geology and history of this incredible natural wonder.

What to Expect on the trip

Guests should come prepared for a physically demanding trek—sturdy hiking boots, a light jacket, plenty of water, and sun protection are essential for this excursion. The terrain varies from rocky paths to uneven slopes, so it's important to wear comfortable clothing suited for the hike. As you ascend, the landscape dramatically shifts from tropical foliage to more barren, volcanic rock, providing a truly surreal experience. Once at the summit, guests will be rewarded with awe-inspiring views of the volcano's active crater and the surrounding mountain range. This expedition offers a unique opportunity to witness the power of nature up close, all while creating lasting memories of Taniti’s untamed beauty.
